Polygon Centroid Point Calculator

Here is the online Polygon Centroid Point calculator to calculate the centroid of polygon area by filling the number of sides of polygon and providing their respective X and Y axis values. Centroid (geometric center) of polygon is the arithmetic mean position of all the points in all of the coordinate directions. Polygon is a two dimensional shape formed by straight lines. Triangles, quadrilaterals, pentagons, and hexagons are some of the polygons.
